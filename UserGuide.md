# LuminusCampus User Guide

A quick guide on how to use the system.

### Getting Started
1.  Log in at `/Account/Login`.
2.  Your dashboard will show you the modules you have access to.

### Roles
*   **Super Admin:** Can manage modules, pages, and client accounts.
*   **Admin:** Manages staff and specific module data.
*   **Staff:** Access is limited to specific pages assigned by the Admin.

### Managing Rights
If you're a Super Admin, use the **Clients** page to assign modules to different users. Once assigned, they'll see those modules in their menu automatically.
