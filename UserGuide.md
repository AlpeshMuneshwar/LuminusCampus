# LuminusCampus User Guide

A quick walkthrough for using LuminusCampus.

## Logging In
1. Open the application in your browser.
2. Enter your email and password on the login page.
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard based on your role.

## Roles

### SuperAdmin
You have full control over the system. Your dashboard gives you access to:
- **Clients** — create Admin accounts and assign module access to them
- **Modules** — view all available ERP modules
- **Pages** — manage individual pages within modules
- **Session Logs** — see who's logged in, from where, and when

### Admin
You manage your assigned modules. Depending on what's been activated for you, you can:
- Add and manage staff, departments, and employees
- Handle academic programs, branches, semesters
- View student records and attendance

### Staff
You can access only the pages your Admin has granted you. Your sidebar menu shows exactly what you have access to — nothing more.

## Managing Access (SuperAdmin only)
1. Go to **Clients** from the sidebar
2. Click on an Admin user
3. Use **Manage Rights** to toggle which modules and pages they can access
4. Changes take effect immediately — the user's menu updates on their next page load

## Themes
The sidebar has theme buttons at the bottom. You can switch between:
- **Light** — clean white background
- **Dark** — easy on the eyes for late-night work
- **Green** — a mint-toned alternative

Your choice is saved in the browser and persists across sessions.

## Sessions
LuminusCampus limits each account to 2 active sessions. If you log in from a third device, the oldest session is automatically terminated. SuperAdmins can view all sessions from the **Session Logs** page.
